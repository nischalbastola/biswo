Our Heritage

Founded in 1985, Bishow Laxmi Jewellery has been a beacon of Nepalese craftsmanship for over three decades. Our journey began in the heart of Kathmandu, where our founder, Mr. Bishow Laxmi, established a small workshop dedicated to preserving traditional jewelry-making techniques.

Today, we continue this legacy by blending time-honored methods with contemporary designs, creating pieces that tell a story of cultural richness and unparalleled artistry.

Each piece in our collection is meticulously handcrafted by our master artisans, ensuring the highest quality and attention to detail that has become synonymous with the Bishow Laxmi name.
